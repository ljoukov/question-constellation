# Biology Chain Family Taxonomy

Temporary validation draft based on the current 75 active/draft Biology exact
answer chains.

The stored topic metadata is mostly `Unknown`, so this taxonomy is based on
chain titles and method shape.

## Summary

- Active/draft exact chains reviewed: 75
- Proposed visible families: 10
- Strongest patterns: stimulus/condition to response, inheritance information,
  data interpretation, practical validity
- Main risk: Biology can easily become topic-led because many mechanisms are
  content-specific. Keep families named as causal or procedural thinking moves.

## Families

Topics represented: homeostasis, respiration, nervous system, hormones,

## Assignment Caveats

Some chains naturally have a primary and secondary family:

- `bio-chain-photosynthesis-rate-gas-per-time`: condition/rate family plus
  quantitative calculation.
- `bio-chain-temperature-control-rate-validity`: condition/rate family plus
  practical validity.
- `bio-chain-incomplete-fossil-evidence-estimate-uncertain`: data limitation
  family plus evaluation family.
- `bio-chain-genetic-similarity-disease-variation-risk`: inheritance/variation
  family plus risk/evaluation family.

Methodology update from this subject: Biology needs an explicit
`condition/signal -> response` family separate from `condition/resource ->
rate/growth`. Without that split, homeostasis, ecology, enzymes, and practical
rate chains become too broad.
