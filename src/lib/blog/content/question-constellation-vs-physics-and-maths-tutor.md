## Quick comparison

| Feature     | Question Constellation                                                             | Physics and Maths Tutor                                                          |
| ----------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Main use    | Question chains, mark checklists and guided transfer between related exam problems | Large free library of past papers, topic questions, notes and revision resources |
| Navigation  | Starts from answer chains and question families                                    | Starts from subject, topic and paper resource lists                              |
| Feedback    | Shows what the answer needs to contain and which reasoning links matter            | Provides resources and mark schemes for self-marking                             |
| Best moment | When a student wants targeted practice on the reason marks are lost                | When a student wants a free archive of practice materials                        |
| Best fit    | Students who want a guided layer over exam-question practice                       | Students and teachers who already know which resource they need                  |

## Resource archive vs answer-chain atlas

Physics and Maths Tutor is valuable because it gives students and teachers access to many resources. The student chooses a subject, paper or topic, then works through material.

Question Constellation takes a narrower route. It asks which chain of reasoning the question rewards and then groups related questions around that chain.

## Why guidance matters

Self-marking with a mark scheme can work, but many students do not know why their answer missed a mark. They may see the official point and think they were close enough.

The answer-chain format makes the missing link visible. It turns a mark scheme into something the student can reuse in the next question.

## Best way to use both

Use PMT when the job is finding papers or topic practice. Use Question Constellation when the job is diagnosing the answer and practising a related question family.

## Verdict

Physics and Maths Tutor is a strong archive. Question Constellation is a guided practice layer. They solve different parts of the GCSE revision problem.
